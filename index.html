Best Answer to "Am I Missing Anything?"

Yes. If you leave out Maintenance, Quality, and Materials / Inventory, your Tier 2 will miss the exact causes that hurt egg flow, packaging stability, and shipping performance.

Best User-Friendly Setup

One shared weekly board, one action rail, one prep form. Keep the meeting simple: review red KPIs, review support needs, assign owners, move on.

Why this color theme

Warm orange and gold pull attention fast, feel energetic, and fit the chicken theme without looking childish. It should get eyes on the board.

✅ Daily Checklist
MARK EACH ITEM PASS OR FAIL — IN ORDER
⏱ ~7h 10m remaining
1
Monitor feed, water & ventilation throughout the shift
⏱ time varies · ongoing
⚠ LOG THE ISSUE & NOTIFY MANAGER
Note the specific system (feed/water/ventilation), any readings, and action taken. Contact manager immediately if feed is powdery, water pressure is low, or ventilation cannot keep up.
2
Full bird check — mortality, chute, sick/loose birds, egg belt & cage damage
⏱ 2 hrs · daily
⚠ LOG ALL ISSUES — NOTIFY MANAGER
Record each issue: mortality count + location, loose birds, sick/injured birds (move to isolation), chute fill level, belt or cage damage. Work orders auto-created for equipment.
🔧 Work Order will be auto-created
3
Water tubes cleaned — front & back of house
⏱ 30 min · daily
⚠ MUST COMPLETE — RETURN TO FINISH
Dirty water tubes cause disease spread. Cannot be skipped. Log which section was missed and finish before end of shift.
4
Front of house cleaned after egg run
⏱ 45 min · daily
⚠ COMPLETE BEFORE SHIFT END
Egg residue and debris at front of house creates food safety risk and fly attractant. Log what was left undone and the reason.
5
Full blow-off — walls, cages, egg collectors & floors (top & bottom of house)
⏱ 3h 30min · daily / every other day
⚠ LOG WHAT WAS MISSED
Buildup on fan screens reduces airflow and raises ammonia. Log which areas were skipped — supervisor will schedule catch-up on next shift.
6
Wheelbarrow emptied & back of house tidied
⏱ 25 min · daily
⚠ DO NOT LEAVE FULL
Wheelbarrow must be emptied before you leave. Log the reason if unable to complete the full back-of-house tidy.
7
Under cage cleaning — all rows, both floors
⏱ 3 hrs · min 2x/week
⚠ LOG WHICH SECTION WAS SKIPPED
Note which cage rows were not cleaned. Supervisor will reschedule for the next available shift to maintain the 2x/week minimum.
8
Designated hallways cleaned (swept & tidied)
⏱ 45 min · every other day
⚠ LOG WHICH HALLWAY WAS SKIPPED
Supervisor will assign catch-up at the start of the next shift. Log which hallway and the reason.
9
Fly check — inspect all traps & bait stations
⏱ 15 min · weekly
⚠ REPORT HIGH ACTIVITY TO MANAGER
Document trap locations with heavy activity. Manager will schedule treatment. Log trap location and estimated activity level.
10
Rodent check — inspect stations, add poison if needed
⏱ 30 min · weekly
⚠ REPORT EVIDENCE TO MANAGER
Document locations where evidence was found. Add poison as needed. Alert manager immediately if evidence found near feed or water systems.
