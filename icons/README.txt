Place your app icons here:

  icon-192.png  — 192x192 px  (required for Android home screen)
  icon-512.png  — 512x512 px  (required for splash screen / PWA install)

Tip: Use the chicken logo from the app header.
You can resize it at https://www.iloveimg.com/resize-image
or use any image editor to export at those exact sizes.

Once the icons are here, the app will be fully installable
on iPad via Safari → Share → Add to Home Screen.
